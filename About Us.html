About Us

Hot Beans Web Development was established in Birmingham, England in 2012 by Tim Milton. The original purpose of Hot Beans was to create an advanced hub where web developers are able to demonstrate their skills through a career at our company.

We target those with a huge passion for web development and look to create a skilled and efficient team who are able to deal with difficult problems they may face and can work together to produce high-quality work.

We also aim to create simplistic and interactive work that provides a high level of satisfaction for all of our clients. Therefore, we value time and effort when working together.

Our friendly team are able to assist with almost anything regarding web development and are open to assisting whenever needed. To learn more about our amazing team, visit our Employee Profiles section of our website. This provides a brief introduction to our current employees for those who may be interested in a career with us.

Additionally, we have recently opened multiple social media accounts for Hot Beans. This is where we will post more about us, and the links can be found in the footer.
