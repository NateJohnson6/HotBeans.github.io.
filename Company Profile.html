Company Profile

Learn more about Hot Beans Web Development

Who We Are

Hot Beans is one of UK's leading web development companies. We offer courses, jobs and information to all users looking to enter this market.

Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Mission: To create and expand a large network of web developers with an advanced skillset.

Vision: To become a well-known company who can offer jobs to those looking to learn web development.

Values: We believe in working as a team and prioritising our customers to produce satisfactory work.

Our History

Founded in 2012, Hot Beans originally began with a small team of novice web developers. Through years of trial and error, our team have developed skills which we would like to teach others. Our team has expanded and developed over the years, producing a larger network within our company.

Achievements and Partnerships

At Hot Beans, we have managed to successfully produce over 200 detailed websites which meet the needs of our customers. In 2017, we were crowned as the 3rd largest web development company in the UK. In the following years, we look to surpass this figure and double the size of our team.
