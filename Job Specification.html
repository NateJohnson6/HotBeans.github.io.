Job Specifications

Explore the exciting job opportunities available at Hot Beans Web Development

Javascript Assistant Developer

Our Javascript Assistant Developers are responsible for checking over any Javascript produced by the team. They are also there to aid with any issues regarding Javascript and provide a quality final product.

Qualifications Required:
  • Degree or equivalent experience in Maths or Computer Science
  • Strong knowledge surrounding HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Completion of Javascript further training course

Assistant Management Director

As our Assistant Management Director, your role is to ensure all of our employees are completing their work to their best standard, and making sure that customer satisfaction is prioritised. Additionally, you need to ensure that employees all recieve the correct wages for the hours they have worked.

Qualifications Required:
  • Degree in Computer Science or Finance
  • Strong understanding of Maths and English
  • Knowledge of database systems involving employee wages and performance

Web Developer

Our Web Developers are in charge of the primary creation of websites for our clients. Their work must be of a high standard and fit the needs of the client.

Qualifications Required:
  • Degree in Computer Science
  • Experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Previous experience with website creation
  • Good problem-solving and debugging skills

CSS Secondary Developer

Our CSS Secondary Developer is tasked to assist our Web Developers with anything relating to CSS. They must also be able to produce simple but effective CSS code which can be taught to others.

Qualifications Required:
  • Degree in Computer Science or Web Development
  • Experience using Notepad++
  • Strong understanding of design principles
  • Ability to collaborate effectively with Managers, Web Developers and clients
